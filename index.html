Zibo Zhao

303 E Superior Street · Chicago, IL 60611 · zibo.zhao@northwestern.edu

I am a research assistant professor at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine. My research focus is the crosstalk of epigenetics and metabolism in human cancers. My goal is to unveil opportunities to sek novel and effective ways of treating cancers bearing certain mutations based on their synthetic lethality profiles. I also have a strong interest in next generation sequencing data analysis and visualization. Learn more about me and my work!
